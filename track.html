Track and Field

Beyond the classroom, a significant portion of my time at UC Berkeley is dedicated to my role as a Varsity Athlete on the California Track and Field team. Specifically, I compete in the Pole Vault—an event that I find perfectly complements my studies in Mechanical Engineering.

The pole vault is essentially a high-speed physics problem. It requires a balance of energy transfer, material mechanics, and precise timing. My background in engineering allows me to approach my training with a technical mindset, while the sport itself has reinforced the importance of discipline, resilience, and attention to detail.
