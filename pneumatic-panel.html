Pneumatic Control Panel

Saturday Robotics Internship

An internship project while working at Saturday Robotics where I designed and manufactured a control panel to conveniently layout and mount a series of pneumatic regulators and switches while maintaining comfortable and accessibility usage and organized tubing management.

Design Phase

The project started with brainstorming a potential layout followed by 3D modeling the system using SolidWorks CAD software to place each of the switches and potential tube routing to ensure a compatible layout.

Manufacturing

I manufactured the mounting plates out of stock material with a laser cutter in the machine shop. The frame that holds up the plate was also modeled in CAD and made out of 80/20 stock metal.
