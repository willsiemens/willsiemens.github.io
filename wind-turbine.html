Wind Turbine

Mechanical Engineering Design Project

The goal of this project was to design and manufacture a functional, small-scale wind turbine capable of converting kinetic wind energy into electrical power. This required a deep dive into aerodynamic blade profiles, gear ratios, and structural stability to maximize efficiency across various wind speeds.

Aerodynamic Geometry

Optimized blade pitch and curvature in SolidWorks to maximize torque at low startup speeds while maintaining structural integrity. The complex blade geometries were manufactured using 3D printing (FDM).

Power Transmission

Integrated a custom drivetrain to translate rotational motion from the turbine hub to the generator shaft efficiently, wiring a DC motor as a generator to stabilize power output.
