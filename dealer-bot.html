Dealer Bot (IN PROGRESS)

Automated Mechatronics System

Designing and building an automated card-dealing robot capable of dispensing playing cards with high precision. This project served as an exercise in integrating moving mechanical assemblies with microcontroller-driven logic.

Mechanical Dispensing

Engineered a friction-based roller system to consistently separate and deal single cards from a standard deck without jamming. The chassis and mounting brackets were modeled in SolidWorks and 3D printed.

Control Systems

Utilized stepper motors and servo mechanisms programmed via microcontrollers to control the speed, trajectory, and rotational distribution of the dealt cards.
