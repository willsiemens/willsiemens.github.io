About Me

I am a mechanical engineering student at the University of California, Berkeley, and a varsity athlete on the Cal Track & Field team. Balancing high-level athletics with a rigorous engineering curriculum has instilled in me a relentless work ethic and the ability to perform under pressure.

I am deeply passionate about how things work. Whether it's finite element analysis, robotic mechatronics, or precision manufacturing, I love taking a project from a conceptual CAD model all the way through physical assembly and testing.

Outside of the classroom and the track, you can find me backpacking and mountain biking with family and friends. Ultimately, I want to leverage my engineering background to design technological advancements that tangibly improve our world.
